Morphine (IV dosing)
• Up to 0.1 mg/kg IV

• MR in additional 5 min at half initial IV dose
Morphine (IM dosing)
• Up to 0.1 mg/kg IM
• MR in 15 min at half initial IM dose
• MR in additional 15 min at half initial IM dose
