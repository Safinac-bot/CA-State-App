## BLS PROCEDURES

• Place patient in cool, shaded environment

• Initiate active cooling measures
  - Remove clothing
  - Fan the patient, or turn on air conditioner
  - Apply ice packs to axilla, groin, back of neck
  - Other active cooling measures as available

• Administer oxygen as indicated

---

## ALS STANDING ORDERS

### ADULT

**IV/IO access**

**Normal Saline**
- IV/IO bolus – 1 Liter
  - Repeat x 1 for persistent hypotension

---

### PEDIATRIC

**IV/IO access**

**Normal Saline**
- IV/IO bolus – 20 mL/kg
  - Max single dose 1 Liter
  - Repeat x 1 for persistent hypotension
