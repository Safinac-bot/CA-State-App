## BLS PROCEDURES

Administer oxygen as indicated

Assess numerical or faces pain scale

Control pain using basic measures such as patient positioning, ice packs, and splinting

---

## ALS STANDING ORDERS

### ADULT

**IV/IO access**

**Cardiac Monitor**

### Mild to Moderate Pain

**Acetaminophen**
- IV/IO – 1g in 100 mL NS over 15 minutes.

### Severe Pain - Utilize opiate analgesic in addition to, or in place of, acetaminophen.

**Fentanyl**
- **IM/IN** - 1 mcg/kg, Max 100 mcg
  - Repeat q 10 minutes for persistent pain to a max total dose of 200 mcg.
- **IV/IO** - 1 mcg/kg over 1 minute, Max 100 mcg
  - Repeat q 5 minutes for persistent pain to a max total dose of 200 mcg.
- Repeat doses should be administered IV/IO if vascular access obtained.
- Withhold for systolic < 90 mmHg.

**If Fentanyl unavailable:**

**Ondansetron** - Per 705.15 Nausea/Vomiting Policy
- Repeat x 1 in 10 minutes for nausea or > 2 doses of Morphine

**Morphine**
- **IM** - 0.1 mg/kg, Max 10 mg
  - Repeat ½ initial dose q 10 minutes x 2 for persistent pain.
- **IV/IO** - 0.1 mg/kg, Max 10 mg, over 1 minute
  - Repeat ½ initial dose q 5 minutes x 2 for persistent pain.

### PEDIATRIC

**IV/IO access**

**Cardiac Monitor**

### Mild to Moderate Pain

**Acetaminophen**
- IV/IO – 15 mg/kg in 100 mL NS over 15 minutes.

### Severe Pain – Utilize opiate analgesic in addition to, or in place of, acetaminophen.

**Fentanyl**
- **IM/IN** - 1 mcg/kg, Max 100 mcg
  - Repeat q 10 minutes for persistent pain to a max total dose of 200 mcg.
- **IV/IO** - 1 mcg/kg over 1 minute, Max 100 mcg
  - Repeat q 5 minutes for persistent pain to a max total dose of 200 mcg.
- Repeat doses should be administered IV/IO if vascular access obtained.
- Withhold for systolic less than minimum SBP in Handtevy for respective age.

**If Fentanyl unavailable:**

**Ondansetron** - Per 705.15 Nausea/Vomiting Policy
- Repeat x 1 in 10 minutes for nausea or > 2 doses of Morphine

**Morphine**
- **IM** - 0.1 mg/kg, Max 10 mg
  - Repeat ½ initial dose q 10 minutes x 2 for persistent pain.
- **IV/IO** - 0.1 mg/kg, Max 10 mg, over 1 minute
  - Repeat ½ initial dose q 5 minutes x 2 for persistent pain.
