## BLS PROCEDURES

Administer oxygen as indicated

## ALS STANDING ORDERS

### ADULT

**IV/IO access**

### Non-Hemorrhagic Shock

**Normal Saline**
- IV/IO bolus – 1 Liter
  - Repeat x 1 for persistent signs of shock

### Hemorrhagic Shock (Atraumatic or Traumatic)

• Judicious volume resuscitation is recommended for hemorrhagic shock of any cause.
  - Target SBP: 90 mmHg
  - Target SBP 65 years and older: 100 mmHg

**Normal Saline**
- IV/IO bolus – 1 Liter
  - Discontinue once target SBP is reached or titrate to sustain target SBP
  - Restart or repeat x1 as needed to sustain target SBP.

**Tranexamic Acid (SBP < 90 mmHg)**
- Refer to Policy 734 for indications and contraindications
- IV/IO - 1g in 100mL NS over 10 minutes

**When Available:**  
**Whole Blood (SBP < 70 or SBP <90 AND HR >110)**
- Warm and rapidly transfuse one unit of low titer O+ whole blood.
- Repeat x 1 when indications return or persist.

---

### PEDIATRIC

**IV/IO access**

### Non-Hemorrhagic Shock

**Normal Saline**
- IV/IO bolus – 20 mL/kg
  - Repeat x 1 for persistent signs of shock

### Hemorrhagic Shock (Atraumatic or Traumatic)

• Judicious volume resuscitation is necessary for hemorrhagic shock of any cause.
  - Target SBP: Handtevy minimum for respective age

**Normal Saline**
- IV/IO bolus – 1 Liter
  - Discontinue once target SBP is reached or titrate to sustain target SBP
  - Restart or repeat x1 as needed to sustain target SBP.

**Tranexamic Acid (SBP less than Handtevy minimum)**
- Refer to Policy 734 for indications and contraindications
- IV/IO - 15mg/kg to a max of 1g in 100mL NS over 10 minutes
